Each dial contains the letters A to Z, each letter on the dial will be assigned a random value ranging from 1 to 6. A certain value will only be assigned to up to 5 or 3 letters.
Example:
A dial could be randomized to be:
ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ

This dial contains 5 ones, 5 twos, 5 threes, 5 fours, 3 fives, and 3 sixes.

104 total letters (26 * 4)
80 are general effects
12 are the worst possible effects, you have a 10% chance to get one
12 are the best possible effects, you have a 10% chance to get one

1st Letter/Dial = Power Output
2nd Letter/Dial = Element
3rd Letter/Dial = Duration of Effect
4th Letter/Dial = Status Effect

First Dial - Power Output:
[Value 1] - 5 - 0 (only affects target)
[Value 2] - 5 - 5ft
[Value 3] - 5 - 10ft
[Value 4] - 5 - 15ft
[Value 5] - 3 - 25ft
[Value 6] - 3 - Dud

Second Dial - Element:
[Value 1] - 5 - Water
[Value 2] - 5 - Earth
[Value 3] - 5 - Fire
[Value 4] - 5 - Air
[Value 5] - 3 - Light
[Value 6] - 3 - Dark

Third Dial - Duration of Effect:
[Value 1] - 5 - 1 turn
[Value 2] - 5 - 2 turns
[Value 3] - 5 - 3 turns
[Value 4] - 5 - 1 round
[Value 5] - 3 - Deathmark (Until an enemy is killed or combat is over)
[Value 6] - 3 - No Duration

Fourth Dial - Status Effect:
[Value 1] - 5 - Slowed (Halved movement speed)
[Value 2] - 5 - Restrained (Enemy will be unable to move)
[Value 3] - 5 - Blinded (Disadvantage on attacks)
[Value 4] - 5 - Weakened (Enemy attacks will do half damage)
[Value 5] - 3 - Empowered (Bomb will deal double damage)
[Value 6] - 3 - Absorbed (Enemy attacks will take on the effects of the bomb)
